# 📊 Sales Data Analysis & Business Insights

## 🚀 Project Overview
This project analyzes transactional retail data to uncover **revenue trends, top-performing products, customer behavior, and regional performance**. The goal is to transform raw data into **clear, actionable business insights** that support decision-making and strategy.

## 🧠 Problem Statement
Businesses generate large volumes of sales data but often fail to extract meaningful insights. This project focuses on identifying revenue drivers, understanding customer value, and highlighting opportunities for growth.

## 📁 Dataset
The dataset contains online retail transactions from **December 2010 to December 2011**, with over **400,000 cleaned records** after preprocessing.

## ⚙️ Data Cleaning & Preparation
Data preprocessing included removing negative quantities (returns), dropping missing customer IDs, converting date formats, and creating new features such as **Revenue (Quantity × UnitPrice)**, as well as extracting **Month** and **Year** for time-based analysis.

## 📊 Analysis Performed
The analysis covers revenue trends over time, top-performing products, regional performance by country, and customer-level revenue contributions to identify high-value customers.

## 📈 Dashboard (Power BI)
An interactive Power BI dashboard was developed featuring monthly revenue trends, top products, country-level performance, customer insights, and KPI cards such as total revenue, total orders, and unique customers.

## 🔑 Key Insights
Revenue peaks toward the end of the year, indicating strong seasonality; a small number of products and customers contribute disproportionately to total revenue; and the United Kingdom dominates overall sales performance.

## 💡 Business Recommendations
Focus on high-performing products, implement retention strategies for top customers, expand efforts in strong markets, and prepare inventory for peak sales periods.

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies
Python (Pandas, Matplotlib), Jupyter Notebook, and Power BI were used for data cleaning, analysis, and visualization.
